About the map layers

Environment overlays

  • Precipitation & temperature — WorldClim 2.1 long-term climate normals for 1970–2000 (mean annual values), clipped to land.
  • Greenness / NDVI — MODIS Terra vegetation index, fixed mid-summer mosaic (July 2010) as a long-term-style snapshot.
  • Elevation — static digital elevation model (no time period).

Why do temp & precip stop at 2000?

They don’t “run out” of years — WorldClim 2.1’s free global climate layers are published as a fixed 1970–2000 30-year normal (Fick & Hijmans 2017). That baseline is still the classroom standard for species distribution work. Later decades exist in other products; this map uses the classic WorldClim normal.
